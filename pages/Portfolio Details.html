Project Story

This mural was created to transform a blank urban wall into a vibrant artistic landmark that reflects the culture and energy of the local community. The concept focused on bold colors, expressive shapes, and storytelling through large-scale visual elements.

The design process included concept sketches, digital mockups, and collaboration with the client to ensure the final mural aligned with the brand identity and the surrounding architecture.

  • Large-scale outdoor mural
  • Weather resistant paints
  • Community inspired design
  • Completed within 7 days
